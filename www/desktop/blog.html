      Since we quietly launched back in June, the team has been tirelessly rolling out new features and end-to-end enhancements on the platform based on feedback from users in our Developer Preview. In addition, the pre-production run and testing of our Connected Touchpoints went very well and I'm pleased to announce that we are officially launching our Connected Touchpoints today. We'll be demonstrating the Connected Touchpoints at the Money Event (booth #6146) in Las Vegas, NV on September 9th to 11th.

      Connected Touchpoints combine advanced NFC and BLE capabilities in one device along with an M2M interface for remote management and monitoring. With Connected Touchpoints and our SDKs, you can deliver consistent user experiences across both Android and iPhone.

      We're also excited to be covered today in VentureBeat.

      We still have more to do to get our open development platform launched to the public, but in the meantime, we are continuing to take requests for our Developer Preview. As mentioned, the Developer Preview requires an NFC phone but you don't have to have any NFC development experience to accomplish some really cool things.
